Resident
Loyalty Program

We are firm believers that happy tenants make for better returns and we have designed a unique Resident Loyalty Program that serves to surprise and delight our resident base. Great residents are an asset both to the community and to real estate owners as they provide:

Greater rental security

Long-term rentals offer landlords a more stable and reliable source of income while avoiding the costs and hassle of frequent tenant turnover.

Reduced turnover costs

Tenant turnover can be costly for landlords, with expenses such as cleaning, repairs, and advertising adding up quickly.

Strong community ties

Longer rental arrangements can foster a sense of community among tenants, resulting in more engaged and invested renters. Tenants who feel connected to their neighbourhoods are more likely to take better care of the property, leading to less damage and maintenance expenses for landlords.

Resident Loyalty Program is designed to curate and cultivate tenant loyalty by aligning incentives and providing structured rewards to motivate tenants to pay on time and over longer durations. We have partnered with SomethingInterestingCo for the program delivery.
